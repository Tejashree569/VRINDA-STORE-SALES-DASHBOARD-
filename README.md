# 📊 Vrinda Store Sales Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

The Vrinda Store Sales Dashboard is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer insights, product categories, and business trends. It enables users to monitor key business metrics through dynamic charts, KPI cards, and slicers.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Track monthly sales trends
- Compare sales across different states
- Identify top-selling product categories
- Analyze customer demographics
- Monitor order status and sales channels
- Support business decision-making using interactive visualizations

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Units Sold
- Total Profit

### Charts
- Top Categories by Sales
- Sales by State
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Channel
- Gender vs Category Mix
- Quantity Sold by Size

### Interactive Filters
- Order Status
- Sales Channel
- Product Category
